About Hale Moku Inn

A family-run boutique inn with deep roots in Lanai City's community.

Our Story

Born from Love of Lanai

Hale Moku Inn was opened in 2008 by Kalei and Ray Akana, a Lanai City couple who wanted to share their beloved island with the world. Lanai had long been overlooked in favor of Maui and Oahu — a hidden gem kept quiet by its lack of commercialization. Ray's family had lived in Lanai City for four generations, and Kalei had moved from Maui when they married.

Together they converted a historic plantation-era home into a boutique inn that honored both Lanai's agricultural heritage and the natural beauty of the island. Every piece of furniture was sourced from the island or made by local craftspeople. The artwork on the walls is by Lanai artists.

Today, Hale Moku Inn is a beloved institution in Lanai City. Guests return year after year, and many have become friends of the family. We believe the best hospitality is simply treating every guest the way you'd want to be treated as a guest in someone's home.
